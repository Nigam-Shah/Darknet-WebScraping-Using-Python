►What is DMT?:

N,N-DMT is an extremely powerful short-acting substance which, when smoked, causes intense visuals and a feeling of "crossing over" to the other side. I can't possibly explain how insane the other side is, but try to imagine the entire world, all of existence, condensed into a single point.

►Where does it come from?:

DMT is a naturally occuring substance found in almost all forms of life, from the pineal gland of the human brain to common grass. It can be extracted from just about anything, though there are few plants that will actually allow for a significant yield. It is believed that DMT is what causes one to dream. It is also believed (and this is just speculation on the part of Dr. Rick Strassman) that DMT is secreted before human death and after human birth. Strassman argues that this may be the cause of the intense visuals people experience during near-death experiences.

►How much are you supposed to take?:

The dosage in the case of DMT is extremely subjective; in other words, no two people are the same when it comes to how much you need to smoke. However, a universally accepted amount is around 40-50mg. This should assure you "crossing over" and experiencing at least most of the potential of the drug. DMT can also be smoked casually, though. Here is roughly what the dosages will feel like:

○10-15mg: very nice LSD/shroom-like visuals for a few minutes

○25-30mg: very strong trip, colorful visuals, however usually not enough to "cross-over".

○35-40mg: This is where you start crossing over, traveling through space and time, encounters with superior beings, witnessing the entire universe at once, etc.

○50-70mg: This is the "heavy", life-changing trip you are trying to achieve. Elves will start talking to you, sharing with you the secrets of the universe, telling you to pay attention to what they have to show and teach you. A truly enlightening trip.
